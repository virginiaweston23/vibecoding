## Strength Priorities

Two sessions per week throughout all phases.

**Glutes & hips**
- Single-leg glute bridges 3×15
- Clamshells with band 3×20
- Lateral band walks 3×15 each direction
- Step-ups 3×12 per leg
- Reverse lunges 3×12 per leg
- Romanian deadlifts 3×10

**Core stability**
- Dead bugs 3×10 per side
- Pallof press 3×12 per side
- Copenhagen planks 3×20 sec
- Plank variations

**Ankle & peroneal (every session)**
- Eccentric calf raises 3×15 (slow lower, 3 sec down)
- Banded ankle eversion 3×15 per side
- Single-leg balance 3×30 sec per side

---

## Injury Prevention — Peroneal Tendon

- Never increase weekly mileage more than 10% from the prior week
- Cutback weeks at weeks 7 and 14 are mandatory
- At the first sign of lateral ankle pain: swap the run for pool running or cycling — do not push through
- In Phase 3, Workout E (Saturday) is the first session to drop if the peroneal flares
- Foam roll peroneals 3x/week
- 10 min mobility after every run: calves, hip flexors, glutes, IT band
